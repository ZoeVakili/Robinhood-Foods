Food That We Give

Our food is donated, but don't worry, we promise it's bacteria free! Before we put our food out on sale, we hire a professional bacteria checker to make sure that the food is clear of any bacteria. We also check our food every few days to make sure that no bacteria builds up overnight. Some food needs certain conditions to stay bacteria free. Like meat, it has to stay cold to keep bacteria out. We spend extra money just to make sure that the food that we give is safe for everyone to eat! We also label the ingredients to make sure that you know whats in the food that you're buying.
